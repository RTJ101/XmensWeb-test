Welcome To The X-MENS Website.

If you are good at something, never do it for free but do it to free your people! 

This page was created to provide updates to the fanbase and support community of Hakkiesdraad Hartman with news, music, videos and events. This website will always be updated whenever new material is available.

Die huidige projek X-MENS is n' kleurvolle album met Frazer Barry as Uitvoerende Vervaardiger, Gaskunstenaars op die album is almal swaargewigte in hulle eie reg en elkeen bied 'n verrykende bydrae  wat die spyskaart geskik  maak vir vir elke smulpaap se smaak kliere. Die eerste enkelsnit Woestyn Blom" (video beskikbaar op youtube) het n aardse tekstuur omring met 'n atmosferiese klankbaan en pols met die hartklop van Frazer Barry se klank ontwerp.

Drie sopranos Zenobia Kloppers, Deniel Barry en Lynelle Kenned strek hulle stembande die hoogtes in wat jou operatiese vervoering neem. Die legendariese Grandmaster Ready D span sy draaitafels in en verryk hierdie musiek skouspel met sy uitmuntende naald-vaardigheid. The award-winning cast on this track alone X-plains the epic proportions of the album "X-MENS'.

Inspirasie in 'n  Lied

In 2018 Hakkiesdraad Hartman was  a Ghoema Nominee in the category for 'Hip-Hop Song of the Year'. The song Surge et Insta" features the George High School choir  and it is also the slogan of the school and the motivation behind the song. It is filled with inspiration and it is reflecting on the principles taught at George High that brought the song to life.  STYG UIT & SKYN.
