Woestyn Blom

This is the first single of the upcoming album 'X-MENS' by Hakkiesdraad Hartman. The track was executively produced by Frazer Georgio Barry and features Zenobia Kloppers, Deniel Barry, Lynelle Kenned and the Grandmaster Ready D on the ones and twos. The video was shot at the Gwaing River Mouth in Pacaltsdorp. The videographers were Nino 'Dirty Fresh' Cleophas, Grand 'EgoNege' Claassen and CJ Levendal. Drone Operators were Stuart McNeal and Elton Olivier. It was edited by NinoFilms and Bo-Menslike Produksies.

"Ek Sal" by Jerome Rex featuring Hakkiesdraad Hartman

Jerome Rex won a Ghoema for Best Hip-Hop Album 2019 and "Ek Sal" is the 2nd single off the Al Jerome album and the song features Hakkiesdraad Hartman, the legendary Cape Town jazz guitarist, Allou April and the track was produced by JK for Exilic Music. The video was shot by Gerald Petersen for Double Dutch Media on location at The Daily Coffee Cafe in Kuils River.

"Opstaan, Ytstaan, Instaan!" by Engelstof & Hakkiesdraad Hartman

SESVOET PRODUCTIONS is proud to present to you our very first official music video. Shot on two locations around Cape Town, named Millers Point Stellenbosch. In this production, a collaboration between ENGELSTOF from Stellenbosch and HAKKIESDRAAD HARTMAN from George (WC) show their amazing skills in Afrikaans Rymklets (Afrikaans RAP). The Track is called "OPSTAAN YTSTAAN INSTAAN", meaning for "A PEOPLE" to stand up and stand out in unity no matter the circumstance. The Call is for people "TO BE THE CHANGE" instead of "WAITING FOR CHANGE"... ONE GOD, ONE AIM, ONE VISION, ONE DESTINY.

Ligte, Kamera, Aksie

Hakkiesdraad Hartman has tried his hand at film-making wrote, produced and directed the scripts for "Woestyn Blom" and "Surge et insta". He also co-produced the videos for "Ek Sê" and "Sê Wat Jy Wil" two video from the collaboration project with SMP(Staff Member Production). There are two videos of the album currently in production for the the songs 'die lewe is mooi' en "x-mens".

From his experiences on movie sets, features in other videos, theatre productions and live recordings he has been nurturing his passion for film making. He has expanded his writing to beyond music and has written one-man shows, comedy skits and short movie scripts which he aims to film as time progresses.
